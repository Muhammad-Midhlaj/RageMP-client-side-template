First of all, you need to earn some initial funds, make you can see what Bony offers, which is on the dock, and namely, going up the stairs, help a woman named Emma ... they continue will accompany you through the storyline, help you earn at least some initial facilities. In the future, for employment, you will need to get to City Hall and after talking with Emma Smith choose one of the available at the initial stages job: Electrician or lawnmower.

Lawnmower

Available: 0 level.

About job: A great opportunity for at least some initial income being a visitor, the work is not dirty, and besides, it does not require licenses or experience, the main thing is to be able to step on the gas and turn the steering wheel in time, mow sitting still, rest and earn.

Salary: 4$ per checkpoint.

Electrician

Available: 0 level.

About Job: Difficult but well-paid job if you are strong in spirit and health, fit physically and are not afraid to horror exhausting hourly runs from the electrical panel to the panel, to correct again and recurring faults, this job is made for you!

Salary: 10$ per chekpoint.

Postal

Available: 1 level.

About Job: One of the professions of the progenitors of all subsequent professions associated with driving or transportation. Requires an approximate knowledge of the area, and the presence of a driver's license of category B (passenger car), due to the fact that movement takes place on public roads, and this requires knowledge of traffic rules.

Salary: 1.700$ per hour of work.

Taxi

Available: 2 level.

About Job: Dream job for those who like to drive and chat with different people. Requires approximate knowledge of the area, the presence of a driving license of category B (passenger cars), due to the fact that movement takes place on public roads, and this requires knowledge of traffic rules

Salary: price of one order from $ 20 to $ 200.

Commands

/tprice [id] [price] - issue a check to a passenger for a taxi ride.

/accept [id] - take a taxi call.

Bus Driver

Available: 2 level.

About Job: This is a profession that requires maximum concentration and good driving skills. To work, you need category "C" rights. Take people along a specific route - from stop to stop.

Salary: 2.000$ per hour of work.

Automechanics

Available: 4 level.

About Job: If you love digging into car engines, understand it and do it quickly - then this job is definitely for you. Repair and refuel cars, making money on it!

Salary: price of one order from $ 50 to $ 300.

Commands

/repair [id] [price] - write a check for repairing a car.

/sellfuel [id] [liter] [price/liter] - write a check for refueling a car.

/buyfuel [id] [liter] - purchase fuel for further refueling.

Truck

Available: 5 level.

About Job: If your body is used to long periods of sleep deprivation and you can also maintain concentration and follow the road, then this job is definitely for you. You must have category "C" rights. Deliver goods according to orders and earn!

Salary: price of one order from $ 500 to $ 2000.

Commands

/orders - open order menu.

/t [message] - truckers walkie talkie.

Collector

Available: 8 level.

About Job: If you are a real man and are not afraid to carry a huge pile of money in the car, then the job is definitely for you. On the way, bandits may try to rob you, if there are more of them, then it is better not to risk it and give them what they demand. For this job you need to have category "C" rights. Deliver bags of money to ATMs and earn!

Salary: 2.500$ per hour of work.

Command Description Available
General

The server has many factions, 15 in total: 5 gangs, 4 mafias, police department, federal bureau of investigation, army, department of health (EMS), government and Los Santos News. Most of the interaction in factions takes place through the menu of interaction with other players using the "G" button

(in this case, you must be aimed at the player with whom you want to interact).

FIB/LSPD

Available: 1 level.

Description of activities: carrying out activities aimed at reducing the level of crime in the country. The Federal Bureau deals with investigations and surveillance, collecting information and working with it, arresting dangerous criminals, etc., while the police perform from the smallest actions like patrolling, fines and licensing, to working together in arrests with the FIB.

List of available buttons / commands:

“U” - on-board computer.

“X” - handcuff a person.

“Z” - lead a person.

/m - using a megaphone.

/su - put a person on the wanted list.

/rfp - release a person from prison.

/incar - drag a man into a car.

/pull - get a man out of a car.

Conditions of employment: Majority, lack of ties with the criminal world, have no criminal record, US citizenship, have an education, honey. health certificate. Excellent physical fitness.
